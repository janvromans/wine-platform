Privacy Policy

How Vinora collects, uses and protects your personal data

Last updated: March 2026

Important: Vinora is currently operated as a pre-launch platform based in the Netherlands. Once a legal entity is established, this policy will be updated with full company details. For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@vinora.eu.

1. Who we are

Vinora ("we", "us", "our") is a wine price comparison platform based in the Netherlands. We operate the website at vinora.eu (and associated subdomains).

As we are currently a pre-launch platform, our legal entity is in the process of being established. In the meantime, Vinora is operated by its founder based in the Netherlands. Once our entity is registered, we will update this policy with our full legal name, registration number (KVK), and registered address.

For all privacy-related matters, you can contact us at: privacy@vinora.eu

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate our platform. Here is an overview:

Data When collected Why Retention
Email address Newsletter signup To send you wine guides and updates Until you unsubscribe
Name, business name, email, country, provider type Provider signup form To process your application to list on Vinora For the duration of the business relationship, or 2 years if application is not progressed
Search queries When you use the search To improve search results and understand popular wines Anonymised after 30 days
Technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) Automatically on all visits Security, performance monitoring, and analytics 90 days

We do not collect payment information, create user accounts, or track your behaviour across other websites.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for newsletter subscriptions. You can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing.
  • Legitimate interests — for search query analysis and technical data, to improve and secure our platform.
  • Pre-contractual necessity — for provider application data, to assess and process your request to join Vinora.

4. How we use your data

We use your data solely for the purposes listed above. We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for automated profiling or decision-making
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Transfer your data outside the European Economic Area (EEA)

5. Third-party services

Vinora uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — our database provider. Data is stored on servers within the EU (Ireland). Supabase is GDPR compliant. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — our website hosting provider. Vercel Privacy Policy →
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — powers our AI sommelier search. Search queries may be processed by Anthropic's API. Anthropic does not store queries beyond the immediate request. Anthropic Privacy Policy →

When you click on a provider link from Vinora's search results, you are directed to an external website. Those websites have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for how they handle your data.

6. Cookies

Vinora currently uses only essential technical cookies necessary for the website to function. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

If we introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and request your consent before setting them.

7. Your rights under GDPR

As a resident of the EU/EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vinora.eu. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. These include:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) on all pages
  • Row-level security on our database
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular review of our data practices

Despite these measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Children's privacy

Vinora is a platform for wine — an alcoholic product. Our services are intended solely for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data through our platform, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Complaints

If you have a complaint about how we handle your data, please contact us first at privacy@vinora.eu. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority:
